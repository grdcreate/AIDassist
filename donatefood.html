Donation Guidelines

Please follow these guidelines to ensure food safety and quality

Temperature Control

Keep hot food hot (>60°C) and cold food cold (<5°C)

Freshness Matters

Donate food prepared within the last 4 hours

Proper Packaging

Use airtight containers to maintain food quality

Avoid These Items

No raw meat, expired items, or opened packages
