Common Questions

Browse through our most frequently asked questions

What types of food can I donate?

We accept non-perishable food items such as canned goods, pasta, rice, and cereal. We also accept fresh produce, dairy, and proteins when we have the capacity to distribute them quickly. Please avoid donating expired items, homemade foods, or items in damaged packaging.

How do I schedule a food donation pickup?

You can schedule a pickup through our website by visiting the "Donate Food" page and filling out the donation form. Our team will contact you within 24 hours to arrange a convenient pickup time. We offer pickup services on weekdays between 9 AM and 5 PM.

Who is eligible to receive food assistance?

We provide food assistance to anyone in need regardless of income level, age, or background. Our services are available to individuals and families facing food insecurity. No documentation is required to receive help from our community food share programs.

How often can I receive food assistance?

You can receive food assistance once per week from any of our distribution centers. We believe in providing consistent support to those in need while ensuring we can serve as many community members as possible.

How can I volunteer with FoodShare?

We welcome volunteers! You can sign up through our website by visiting the "Volunteer" page. We have opportunities for food sorting, distribution assistance, administrative support, and community outreach. Both individual and group volunteering options are available.

Do I need any special skills or training to volunteer?

No special skills are required for most volunteer positions. We provide all necessary training on-site. For certain roles like food safety handling, we offer brief training sessions to ensure everyone follows proper protocols.

How many people does FoodShare help each month?

Last month, we provided food assistance to over 5,000 individuals and families across our community. Since our founding, we've distributed more than 2 million meals to those in need. You can see our current impact metrics on our "Our Impact" page.

Where does the donated food go?

Donated food is distributed through our network of community centers, schools, and partner organizations. We prioritize getting food to areas with the highest need, including low-income neighborhoods, senior centers, and shelters. 98% of all donations go directly to people in need.

Is FoodShare a nonprofit organization?

Yes, FoodShare by AIDassist is a registered 501(c)(3) nonprofit organization. All donations are tax-deductible to the extent allowed by law. Our financial reports are available publicly on our website for transparency.

How can I make a monetary donation?

You can make a monetary donation through our website on the "Donate Now" page. We accept credit cards, PayPal, and bank transfers. You can also mail a check to our headquarters. Monetary donations help us cover operational costs and purchase specific needed items in bulk.
