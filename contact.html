Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions about FoodShare

What types of food can I donate?

We accept non-perishable foods, fresh produce, baked goods, and prepared meals that meet our safety guidelines. We cannot accept expired items, homemade canned goods, or anything that has been opened.

How do I schedule a food pickup?

You can schedule a pickup through our website, mobile app, or by calling our hotline. We offer pickup services Monday through Saturday during business hours.

Who receives the donated food?

Our partner organizations include homeless shelters, food pantries, community centers, and low-income housing facilities. We also work directly with families in need through our outreach programs.

How can I volunteer with FoodShare?

We welcome volunteers for food collection, sorting, and distribution. You can apply through our website, attend an orientation session, and choose shifts that fit your schedule.
