How It Works

Donating your surplus food is simple and makes a huge impact

1

Pack Your Surplus Food

Package your leftover food in containers. We accept homemade meals, restaurant surplus, and packaged goods.

Packing Guide
2

Schedule a Pickup

Use our app or call our hotline to schedule a pickup at your convenience. We'll come to your location.

Schedule Now
3

We Collect & Inspect

Our trained volunteers collect the food, check for quality and safety, then transport it to our centers.

Learn More
4

Distribute to Those in Need
